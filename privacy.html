Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Inner Orbit respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Inner Orbit does not collect personal data for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

The information you enter into the app, such as habits, reminders, streaks, and progress data, is stored locally on your device.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Inner Orbit uses local notifications on your device. Notification data is not sent to external servers.

Data Sharing

Inner Orbit does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to back up your device through Apple services such as iCloud backups.

Third-Party Services

Inner Orbit does not use third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services.

Children’s Privacy

Inner Orbit does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can delete your app data at any time by removing the app from your device or by resetting data within the app if that feature is available.

You can also disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised update date.
